Ilya "m0NESY" Osipov (born May 1, 2005) is a Russian professional Counter-Strike 2 player who plays for Team Falcons as the AWPer and a former Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player. m0NESY was named Rookie of the Year in 2022, his professional debut, by HLTV and placed seventh in their annual player rankings. [1][2] He was later ranked the fourth best player of 2023 and the second best of 2024. [3][4] He is known for his explosive AWPing and swift flick shots.
